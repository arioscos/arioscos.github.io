MeetAri

Ari Garcia Oscos is an aspiring new saxophone and piano player based in Tucson hoping to move to Los Angeles soon!

As a University of Texas Longhorn, he has played on numerous events, restaurants, and bars in Austin. Ari is also a former member of the Carribean Jazz Band formed by Dr. Moore in Austin
